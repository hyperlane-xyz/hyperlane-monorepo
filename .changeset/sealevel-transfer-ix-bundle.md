The Sealevel warp adapters now expose a Sealevel-only `getTransferRemoteIxBundle({ ..., clientSalt? })` (and `getTransferRemoteToIxBundle` on the cross-collateral adapter) that returns the building blocks of a `transfer_remote` tx тАФ compute-budget ixs separated from the warp ixs, ALTs, fee payer, and signers тАФ without compiling them into a `Transaction` / `VersionedTransaction`. The cross-VM `ITokenAdapter` interface is unchanged.
