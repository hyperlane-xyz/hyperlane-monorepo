`SealevelQuotedTransferProvider` ships as the Sealevel implementation of `QuotedTransferProvider`. It composes a single atomic tx that prepends `SubmitFeeQuote` (and, when configured, `SubmitIgpQuote`) instructions onto the Sealevel adapter's `transfer_remote` / `transfer_remote_to` ix bundle — `[...computeBudgetIxs, submitFeeQuote, submitIgpQuote?, transferRemote]`. Atomicity is required for transient mode (one-shot PDA) and uniform for standing mode. Constructor accepts a `mode` (`'standing' | 'transient'`), the v2 fee-quoting client, the SVM `Connection`, fee program / account refs, and optional IGP refs (skip the IGP submit ix until the on-chain SVM IGP supports `SubmitIgpQuote`). Standing mode sends `ZERO_BYTES32` as the API salt; transient generates a random raw client salt and derives `scopedSalt = keccak256(payer || rawSalt)` for both the on-chain PDA cascade and the IGP transient-quote PDA. A companion `composeSealevelTx` helper takes a flat ix list + ALTs + fee payer and emits either a legacy `Transaction` or a v0 `VersionedTransaction`, depending on whether ALTs are configured.
